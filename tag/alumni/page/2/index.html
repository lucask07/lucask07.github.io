Alumni

Matthew Schewe

Matthew was a UST Electrical Engineering Major who graduated in the Spring of 2020. He worked on electronics for a new ion-channel amplifier. He is a multisim expert and finished an entire PCB design cycle in the summer of 2019.

Mohamed Abdalla

Mohamed is a UST Master’s student in the graduate programs in software. He is working on an independent study project to develop inexpensive methods to calibrate and evaluate accelerometers and gyroscopes (IMUs).

Ryan Jans

Ryan graduated from UST with an Electrical Engineering Major in the spring of 2020. He did excellent work on optical time-of-flight depth sensors and was the first undergraduate to be the first author on a reviewed journal paper in our lab Characterization of a Miniaturized IR Depth Sensor With a Programmable Region-of-Interest That Enables Hazard Mapping Applications.

Phillip Kuplic

Phil is a UST Mechanical Engineering Major who graduated in the Spring of 2020. He constructed an apparatus for characterizing LED illumination systems for microscope and spectrophotometer applications. He presented his work on LED characterization at the APS March meeting in Boston in 2019.

Savannah Johnson

Savannah graduated in the Spring of 2020 with a major in Mechanical Engineering and minors in Electrical Engineering and Aerospace Studies. Along the way she won 3 national awards as a ROTC cadet She is curious about the real-world applications of physics and mathematics.

Lucas Manke

Lucas graduated in the Spring of 2020 and was a senior majoring in Mechanical Engineering and pursuing minors in Business Administration and Military Science. He was a member of the varsity swim team at UST and a cadet in Army ROTC at the University of Minnesota.
